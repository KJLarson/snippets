```js
var foo == bar;
var foo === bar;
```

Operator | Comparison
| --- | --- |
==	| Equal
===	| Strict Equal
!=	| Not Equal
!==	| Strict Not Equal
<	| Less Than
>	| Greater Than
<=	| Less Than or Equal
>=	| Greater Than or Equal
