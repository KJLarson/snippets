Operator | Comparison
| --- | --- |
`==`	| Equal
`===`	| Strict Equal
`!=`	| Not Equal
`!==`	| Strict Not Equal
`<`	| Less Than
`>`	| Greater Than
`<=`	| Less Than or Equal
`>=`	| Greater Than or Equal

```js
var foo == bar;
var foo === bar;
```
